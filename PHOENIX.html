ABILITIES

  • Hot Hands:

    Throw a fireball that explodes after a delay or upon impact with the ground. The fire zone damages enemies but will also heal you.

  • Curveball:

    Cast a curving flare that bursts into brilliant light after a brief delay, temporarily blinding all looking at it a la flashbang. FIRE will curve the ball to the left while ALT. FIRE will curve it to the right.

  • Blaze:

    Summon a flame wall that blocks vision and damages anyone passing through it. You can bend the wall when casting by holding FIRE and moving your crosshair

  • Run it back:

    Mark your current location. If you die during this ability’s duration, or when this ability’s duration expires, you’ll be reborn at the marked location with full health.


    • Pheonix Tips and Tricks

  • Be aggressive - Pheonix is the perfect entry-fragger, blind your opponents, get in their face and get the kills.
  • Blaze is like a super Molotov, not only will it damage enemies it will heal you. Use it, push them back and go in for the kill.
  • Run it back is good when playing as the entry fragger or when pushing into a Spike site - but be careful be far back enough that when you respawn you are not quickly moped up by the opponent who has killed your replica.
