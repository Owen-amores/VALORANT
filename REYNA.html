  • Dismiss:

    This ability will instantly consume a nearby Soul Orb, becoming intangible for a short duration. If EMPRESS is active, also become invisible.

  • Leer:

    This ability allows Reyna to equip an ethereal, destructible eye. Activating it will cast the eye a short distance forward, and will Nearsight all enemies who look at it.

  • Devour:

    Any enemies killed by Reyna drop 3 Soul Orbs. These are instantly consumed when nearby, and will rapidly heal for a short duration. Health gained through this skill exceeding 100 will decay over time. If EMPRESS is active, this skill will automatically cast and not consume Soul Orbs.

  • Empress:

    Instantly enter a frenzy, increasing firing, equip and reload speed dramatically. Gain infinite charges of Soul Harvest abilities. Scoring a kill renews the duration.
